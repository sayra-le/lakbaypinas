Destinations Recommendation

Baguio City


Known as the "Summer Capital of the Philippines," is a cool, mountainous city located in northern Luzon. Famous for its pine trees, fresh strawberries, and scenic views, it offers a refreshing escape from the tropical heat. Popular attractions include Burnham Park, Mines View Park, and the vibrant Session Road.

Boracay Island


Famous for its powdery white sand and crystal-clear waters, Boracay Island is a top tropical destination in the Philippines. It offers vibrant nightlife, thrilling water activities, and relaxing beach vibes, especially along the iconic White Beach.

Mt. Apo


Standing as the highest peak in the Philippines, Mt. Apo is a majestic stratovolcano located in Davao. It is a haven for hikers and nature lovers, offering challenging trails, rich biodiversity, and breathtaking views from the summit.

THE PHILIPPINES

The Philippines is an archipelagic country located in Southeast Asia, made up of over 7,000 islands. It is known for its rich culture, beautiful beaches, and diverse landscapes. The country was a Spanish colony for more than 300 years, which strongly influenced its language, religion (predominantly Christianity), and traditions. It later came under American rule before gaining full independence in 1946. The Philippines has a democratic government, a fast-growing economy, and a warm, friendly population. Its capital is Manila, and its national language is Filipino, alongside English.
