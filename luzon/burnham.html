City Park in Baguio City

Burnham Park is a popular destination in Baguio City, known for its peaceful atmosphere and scenic lake.

It’s a perfect spot for relaxation and family outings in the heart of the city.

WHY VISIT
BURNHAM PARK

Burnham Park is one of Baguio City’s most iconic and loved destinations, offering visitors a tranquil escape amidst the cool mountain air. Located in the heart of the city, it spans over 30 hectares and features a man-made lake where tourists can enjoy rowing boats while taking in the scenic views. The park's lush greenery and tree-lined pathways create a peaceful ambiance, making it a perfect spot for relaxation and leisurely walks..

In addition to the lake, Burnham Park boasts several other attractions, such as picnic areas, playgrounds, and a beautiful rose garden. Visitors can rent bikes, paddle boats, or even explore the park on foot while admiring the surrounding flowers and trees. The park is especially popular for family outings and is often filled with locals and tourists enjoying the refreshing weather of Baguio.

The park is named after Daniel Burnham, the urban planner who helped design Baguio’s layout during the American colonial period. It is one of the oldest and most recognized public spaces in the city, offering a glimpse of Baguio’s historical and cultural heritage. Whether it's for a quiet afternoon, a fun family day, or a chance to connect with nature, Burnham Park remains a central and beloved part of Baguio’s charm.

Activities Your Must Try

Boating sa Burnham Lagoon
Rent a rowboat or swan boat and paddle across the scenic man-made lagoon at the heart of the park. It's a relaxing way to enjoy the cool Baguio breeze and picturesque surroundings.
Biking Around the Park
Explore the park by renting a bicycle or go-kart. Whether you're with family or friends, it's a fun and active way to see the park's beauty.
Strolling Through the Rose Garden
Take a leisurely walk through the Rose Garden, adorned with vibrant blooms and offering a peaceful atmosphere. It's a perfect spot for relaxation and photography.
Visiting the Children's Playground
Let the kids enjoy the playground equipped with slides, swings, and other fun structures. It's a safe and enjoyable area for children to play and make new friends
