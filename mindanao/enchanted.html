ENCHANTED RIVER


Tourist attraction in Hinatuan in the Philippines.

The Enchanted River is a stunning natural river with vibrant sapphire-blue waters that seem to glow under the sunlight.

It is surrounded by lush forest, creating a magical and peaceful atmosphere.

The river is famous for its unexplored depths and legends about spirits guarding it.

WHY VISIT
ENCHANTED RIVER

Enchanted River is one of the most popular tourist spots in Mindanao due to its unique, crystal-clear waters that appear to be lit from beneath. It is a deep spring river that flows directly into the Pacific Ocean. Many believe the river is enchanted because of its unmeasurable depth and the sudden change in water color from light turquoise to deep blue.

Locals say the river is protected by mystical beings, which adds to the allure of the place. There is even a daily "feeding time" when tourists are asked to step away as fish come to the surface, and music plays in the background. This spectacle adds a cultural and magical feel that fascinates visitors from all over the world.

Surrounded by limestone cliffs and thick forest, Enchanted River is a peaceful getaway for nature lovers. The site is well-preserved to maintain its natural beauty and cleanliness. Visitors can enjoy several eco-friendly activities and learn more about the river’s history and local folklore.
