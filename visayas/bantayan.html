BANTAYAN ISLAND


Tropical Island Paradise in the Philippines.

Bantayan Island is a tranquil beach haven with stunning white sand and turquoise waters.

Located in northern Cebu, it offers a peaceful escape away from city crowds.

Its natural beauty and local charm attract both local and foreign tourists.

WHY VISIT
BANTAYAN ISLAND

Bantayan Island is one of Cebu’s top island destinations, ideal for tourists seeking serenity and sea breeze. Its laid-back atmosphere, friendly locals, and untouched beaches make it a perfect tropical escape. The island has a rustic charm that blends traditional Filipino culture with natural beauty.

Popular beaches like Kota Beach and Paradise Beach feature calm waves and picture-perfect views. Tourists often explore nearby islets, sandbars, and fish sanctuaries while enjoying the peaceful surroundings. Sunsets in Bantayan are spectacular, making evenings just as memorable as the day.

Beyond the beach, Bantayan Island also offers historical and cultural attractions. You can visit old churches, local markets, and try delicious seafood freshly caught by island fishermen. It’s a destination that blends relaxation, adventure, and cultural immersion.

Activities Your Must Try

Beach Hopping
Explore pristine beaches like Kota Beach, Sugar Beach, and Paradise Beach.
Island Hopping
Visit nearby islands like Virgin Island or Hilantagaan for snorkeling and sightseeing.
Skydiving
For thrill-seekers, Bantayan offers one of the few skydiving experiences in the Philippines.
Biking or Motorbiking Around the Island
Rent a bike or motorbike and explore the island’s quiet towns and scenic roads at your own pace.
