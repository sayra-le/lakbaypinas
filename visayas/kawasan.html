WHY VISIT
KAWASAN FALLS

Kawasan Falls is a breathtaking natural wonder tucked in the mountains of Badian, Cebu. Its vibrant blue waters come from mountain springs and flow through multiple cascading levels. The area is a popular stop for tourists looking for a refreshing dip and unforgettable scenery.

The first waterfall is the largest and most accessible, where visitors can swim, raft, or simply relax. The upper levels are reachable by a short hike and are less crowded, offering a more serene experience. Each level has its own natural pool, surrounded by lush tropical foliage and limestone walls.

Aside from its beauty, Kawasan Falls is also known for adrenaline-pumping adventures like canyoneering. This thrilling activity involves jumping from cliffs, sliding down rocks, and swimming through gorges. With its combination of calm and excitement, Kawasan Falls appeals to all kinds of travelers.

Activities Your Must Try

Canyoneering

Swimming
Enjoy a refreshing dip in the cold, turquoise waters of the main waterfall pool.

Ride a bamboo raft and get a natural water massage right under the waterfall.
